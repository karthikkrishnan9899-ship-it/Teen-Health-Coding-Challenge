# Teen-Health-Coding-Challenge
Teen Health Dataset
•	Average social media use = 4.5 hours/day → quite high 
•	Sleep averages 6.45 hours → below recommended levels for teens 
•	Stress, anxiety, addiction levels are all around 5.4 (moderate) 
•	Depression label is very low (2.5%) → dataset is highly imbalanced 
•	Gender wise both Male and Female are almost equal
•	Many students: 
o	Use screens 1.74 hours before sleep 
o	Have low physical activity (1 hour/day) 
•	Limit daily social media time from 4.5 hours to 2 to 3 hours
•	Improve sleep duration and timing to 7 to 8 hours and have a track on it
•	Reduce screen exposure before sleep and stop screening at least 1 hour before sleeping
•	Increase outdoor games and physical activities 60 to 90 min/daily
